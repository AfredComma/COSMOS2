4. Writing WorkflowsÂ¶

Cosmos has a powerful syntax for describing and construction workflows. Workflows are stored and visualized as a DAG who’s nodes are tasks (or jobs) and who’s edges are job dependencies.

Be sure to spend a lot of time with the examples, as this is often the easiest way to learn.
